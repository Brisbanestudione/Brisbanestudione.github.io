What is AMEB?

The AMEB offers a wide range of music examinations for various instruments, including piano, guitar, violin, cello, voice, and many more. These examinations provide a structured and comprehensive assessment of a student's musical skills and knowledge, and are widely recognized by universities, conservatories, and music schools across Australia and internationally.

Course 1

Basic music theory: We introduce students to the foundational concepts of music theory, such as reading music notation, understanding rhythms and time signatures, and basic music terminology.

Course 3

Piano technique: We focus on developing students' piano technique, including hand position, posture, and fingering. We provide exercises and drills that help students to develop finger strength, dexterity, and flexibility.

Course 2

Repertoire: We introduce students to a range of music that is appropriate for their level, including classical, pop, and folk music. We help students to develop their skills in playing music by ear, improvising, and arranging.
